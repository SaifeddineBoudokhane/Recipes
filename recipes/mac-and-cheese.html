Homemade Macaroni and Cheese Mix

Description

Creamy macaroni and cheese is the ultimate comfort food, featuring tender macaroni enveloped in a velvety cheese sauce.
Baked to perfection, it boasts a golden crust on top for a delightful crunch.
This classic dish is a crowd-pleaser, offering a comforting blend of rich flavors and satisfying textures. Perfect as a side or a main course, it's a timeless favorite that brings joy to any table.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups (6 ounces/170g) dry macaroni, shells, or other smaller pasta shape
  • 3 tablespoons (20g) cheddar cheese powder, orange or white
  • 1/2 teaspoon tapioca starch, optional
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3 tablespoons milk, any kind
  • 1 to 2 tablespoons butter (if using salted, reduce the salt a bit)
